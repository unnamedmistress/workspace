# PermitPath Vision Document

## Executive Summary

PermitPath is an AI-powered permit documentation platform designed specifically for contractors in Pinellas County, Florida. Our mission is to eliminate the paperwork chaos that costs contractors hours of lost productivity and delays project starts by an average of 2-3 weeks.

---

## 1. Problem Statement

### The Current Reality for Contractors

Permit requirements in Pinellas County are fragmented across multiple systems:
- **Pinellas County Building Department** (primary permits)
- **Municipal authorities** (St. Pete, Clearwater, etc. have additional requirements)
- **Florida Department of Business & Professional Regulation** (state-level requirements)
- **HOA/condo associations** (additional documentation layers)

### Why Current Solutions Fail

| Current Approach | Why It Fails |
|-----------------|--------------|
| **Calling the permit office** | Hold times of 30+ minutes; staff give conflicting information |
| **PDF checklists from county website** | Static documents that don't account for project specifics; updated infrequently |
| **Hiring permit expediters** | $200-500 per permit; creates dependency; not scalable |
| **Wing it and fix later** | Rejection rates of 40-60%; $150+ re-review fees; weeks of delays |
| **Generic project management tools** | Don't understand permit workflows; require manual setup |

### The Hidden Costs

For a typical roofing contractor doing 20 jobs/month:
- **Time waste**: 8-12 hours/month on permit research and documentation
- **Rejection costs**: $3,000-6,000/month in re-review fees and delays
- **Opportunity cost**: 2-3 week delays mean fewer jobs completed per season
- **Compliance risk**: Missing required inspections can void insurance/warranties

## 3. Core Value Proposition

### One-Sentence Value Prop

**"PermitPath tells you exactly what documents you need, helps you prepare them correctly, and tracks your permit through approval—so you get to work faster."**

### Three Pillars

#### 1. Smart Requirements Engine
Instead of static checklists, PermitPath uses AI to understand:
- Job type + scope + location = specific requirements
- Local amendments to Florida Building Code
- HOA/condo documentation needs
- Current permit office preferences (what they're actually enforcing)

**User experience:** Answer 5-7 questions about the job, get a tailored checklist.

#### 2. Document Intelligence
- Auto-generate permit drawings from photos/measurements
- Pre-fill forms with job details
- Validate documents before submission ("Your insurance cert expires next week—update it first")
- Convert phone photos to acceptable permit documentation

#### 3. Permit Lifecycle Tracking
- Real-time status updates (not just "pending")
- Inspection scheduling and reminders
- Renewal alerts for expiring permits
- Historical record for warranty/insurance

## 5. Practical AI Features (No Gimmicks)

### Phase 1: Intelligence Layer (MVP)

**Smart Requirements Assistant**
- Natural language job description → structured requirements
- Example: "Replace roof on 2,000 sq ft house in St. Pete" → specific checklist
- Confidence scoring: "We found 12 requirements; 3 may apply based on your answers"

**Document Validator**
- OCR + validation of permit documents
- Checks: expiration dates, signatures, correct forms, completeness
- Flags issues before submission

**Smart Forms**
- Pre-fill known data (business info, license numbers)
- Convert measurements ("24 squares" → square footage)
- Generate narrative descriptions from structured inputs

### Phase 2: Automation Layer (v2)

**Photo-to-Drawing**
- Upload job site photos → generate permit sketch
- Basic roof diagrams, site plans from phone photos
- Not CAD-level, but "permit office acceptable"

**Submission Assistant**
- Package documents in county-required format
- Generate transmittal letters
- Track submission confirmation

**Inspection Prep**
- Generate inspection checklists based on permit type
- Reminders for required documentation on-site
- Post-inspection note capture

### Phase 3: Intelligence Network (v3)

**Permit Intelligence**
- Aggregate anonymized data on approval times by permit type
- "Permits like yours are taking 5-7 days currently"
- Early warning on new requirements ("Pinellas is now requiring X for all roofing")

**Predictive Scheduling**
- Recommend optimal inspection timing
- Weather-aware rescheduling suggestions

## 9. Technical Architecture Principles

### Build for the Field
- Mobile-first design
- Offline capability for checklist access
- Fast upload even on spotty connections
- Voice input for hands-free use

### AI as Augmentation, Not Replacement
- AI suggests, human confirms
- Confidence scores on all AI outputs
- Easy override for all automated decisions
- Clear explanation of why something is required

### Data as Moat
- Every permit improves the system
- Anonymized learnings benefit all users
- No data lock-in: users can export everything

### Compliance First
- SOC 2 Type II for data security
- Document retention per Florida law
- Audit trail for all permit activities
