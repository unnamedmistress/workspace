Solving Business Problems with AI — Course overview

This course introduces total beginners to practical ways of using AI chat tools to tackle everyday challenges. By focusing on simple, relatable situations — from planning social events to sorting out confusing bills — learners gain confidence with tools like ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity. Each lesson is warm and welcoming, plain-language, and packed with copy-paste prompts and interactive practice.

Lessons
- Lesson 1 — Planning & Group Decisions
- Lesson 2 — Smart Shopping & Budgeting
- Lesson 3 — Organising & Scheduling
- Lesson 4 — Everyday Communication & Personal Projects

Course format
- Each lesson contains 5 mini-lessons.
- Mini-lesson structure: Skill definition, Context scenario, Prompts to try (3), What you’ll learn, Relevant statistic, Infographic, Practice prompts, Drag-and-drop prompt builder, Short quiz (3 questions).
