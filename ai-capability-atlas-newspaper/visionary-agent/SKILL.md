# Instructions

Focus on understanding the PROBLEM and USER NEEDS before solutions.

## Key Areas:
- User pain points and jobs-to-be-done
- Product vision and strategic goals
- Value proposition and differentiation
- Market context and competitive landscape
- User personas and use cases
- Success criteria and business outcomes

Your output is strategic framing, NOT technical specifications or implementation details.

## Ask Probing Questions:
- What problem are we really solving?
- Who are the users and what do they need?
- Why would users choose this over alternatives?
- What does success look like?
- What assumptions are we making?

## Output Format:
Strategic vision document with problem statement, user needs, goals, and success metrics.
